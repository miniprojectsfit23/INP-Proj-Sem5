Japan

Japan (Japanese: 日本) is an island country in East Asia, located in the northwest Pacific Ocean. It is bordered on the west by the Sea of Japan, and extends from the Sea of Okhotsk in the north toward the East China Sea and Taiwan in the south. Part of the Ring of Fire, Japan spans an archipelago of 6852 islands covering 377,975 square kilometers (145,937 sq mi); the five main islands are Hokkaido, Honshu, Shikoku, Kyushu, and Okinawa. Tokyo is Japan's capital and largest city; other major cities include Yokohama, Osaka, Nagoya, Sapporo, Fukuoka, Kobe, and Kyoto.

Japan is the eleventh-most populous country in the world, as well as one of the most densely populated and urbanized. About three-fourths of the country's terrain is mountainous, concentrating its population of 125.36 million on narrow coastal plains. Japan is divided into 47 administrative prefectures and eight traditional regions. The Greater Tokyo Area is the most populous metropolitan area in the world, with more than 37.4 million residents.

How to Get There


    -How to Reach Japan by Sea
    Another way to travel to Japan from India is by taking a cruise ride via sea. However, there is no direct cruises available from India. One needs to get to Singapore or China first and then take a cruise ride which will take you to Japan. It is time consuming but offers a great luxurious experience to the travelers.

    -How to Reach Japan by Air
    The only and the best way to reach Japan is by air. A number of connecting as well as direct flights operate from India to Japan. The major airports in Japan are Osaka International Airport, Narita Airport, Haneda and Kansai Airports. Most of the flights arrive at Haneda Airport in Tokyo from India. India to Japan flight distance is approx. 5968 km and it takes around 5 to 6 hours to reach Japan from India. Direct flight connectivity is from Delhi only whereas you will get connecting flights from other cities like Chennai, Bangalore, Mumbai etc.

    -How to Reach Japan by Road
    Being located in the Pacific Ocean and being an island country, it is not possible to reach Japan by road from India. The only way to get there is by taking a flight and it is also the most convenient method to reach Japan. The inter road connectivity in Japan is pretty good and cabs and local buses can be hired for commuting within Japan.
